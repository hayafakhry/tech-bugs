Hello! I'm Haya Fakhry from Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Welcome to my personal documentation space where I share my learning journey in Information Technology.

This website was created as a knowledge repository for IT Networking and Cloud Computing fundamentals. As someone passionate about technology, I believe in learning by documenting and sharing knowledge with others.
